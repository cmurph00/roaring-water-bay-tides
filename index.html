Method: Ringaskiddy (Cork Harbour) harmonic predictions stand in for the official standard port, Cobh, 1.3km away. Baltimore's time offset (−4 min high tide, −15 min low tide) and heights (via the official Admiralty ratio method, using Cobh's MHWS 4.1m / MHWN 3.2m / MLWN 1.3m / MLWS 0.4m and Baltimore's own MHWS 3.5m / MHWN 2.9m / MLWN 1.4m / MLWS 0.6m) were validated against real app and Marine Institute data for 14–20 July 2026: high tide within ~5 min, low tide within ~15–20 min, heights within 5–10cm.

The time offset is a flat value, not a full spring/neap curve, so accuracy outside that validated window — especially near a neap trough — is unconfirmed and may be worse. Planning use only. Never use for navigation.
