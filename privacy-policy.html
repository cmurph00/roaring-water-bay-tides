Privacy Policy — Ireland's Tides (RWB Tides)

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Ireland's Tides is an offline tide-prediction app for the Irish coast. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle. In short: the app collects no personal data and transmits nothing about you anywhere.

Data we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that collect data. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Location

If you tap “Use my location”, the app asks Android for your approximate (coarse) location. This is used only on your device, once, to pick the nearest tide-prediction station. Your location is never stored, logged, or sent to us or to any third party. You can decline the permission and still use the app by searching or picking a spot on the map. The app does not use precise/GPS location and does not access location in the background.

Data stored on your device

The app saves small preferences locally (your last-viewed station, day-count, light/dark theme, list/map view) in the browser/app storage on your device only. This never leaves your device and is removed if you clear the app's data or uninstall it.

Network

The app is offline-first: tide predictions and map data are bundled in the app. It does not send your data anywhere. (If a future version offers an optional online refinement, this policy will be updated before that ships.)

Tide & map data sources

Predictions and map layers are built from open data — Marine Institute, EPA, and Tailte Éireann / Ordnance Survey Ireland (CC-BY 4.0), GeoNames (CC-BY), NOAA and TICON-4, and Natural Earth (public domain). The app is for planning only and must not be used for navigation.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
